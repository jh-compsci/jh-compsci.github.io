Our Partners

CocoRobo

We parntered with CocoRobo to create and delivered an engaging, hands-on AI and mathematical modelling workshop series specially designed for primary school students. Through interactive games, storytelling, colorful visual tools, and simple coding activities, the students explored basic concepts such as patterns, logic, probability, and how computers can “learn” from examples. The course sparked curiosity and confidence in STEM subjects, with many students enthusiastically applying what they learned to their own creative projects, helping lay a strong foundation for future learning and inspiring the next generation of problem solvers.

Tung Wah College

We developed an advanced simulation and optimization platform to improve elevator scheduling and energy efficiency for their campus building. By applying sophisticated mathematical modeling, we optimized traffic flow patterns and reduced average wait times by over 28% in peak time. This project not only elevated their user experience but also contributed to their sustainability goals through measurable reductions in energy consumption across deployed installations.

Macau Fly Co. and Musjoy Co.

We partnered with Macau Fly Co. and Musjoy Co. to modernize their software systems. Our team conducted a comprehensive audit and implemented a scalable, cloud-native architecture using modern frameworks and microservices. The result was an improvement in system performance, significantly reduced maintenance costs, and enhanced reliability that supported their rapid expansion into new markets.
