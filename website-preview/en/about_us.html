Gifted Education

Professor Hon had initiated a pioneering university-wide project to develop university short courses for students of the Hong Kong Academy of Gifted Education (HKAGE). The programme, entitled “Talent, Aspiration, and Excellence (TAE)”, was funded by the HKAGE and had run for two years in 2017 and 2018. The short courses of TAE covered different subjects including mathematics, solar energy, mechanical motion animation, English communication, and business strategy. Affective education was incorporated in the TAE programme to enhance emotional regulation of students. A total of about 150 students of the HKAGE participated in the TAE with very good outcomes according to feedbacks from both HKAGE and the participants.
